Lines and Linear equations

In mathematics, a line is an object which is infinitely long and completely straight. Lines are universally used in game development because they represent many common phenomena.

There are many ways to represent a line, but in the most common representation with one variable is called the slope-intercept form, and it looks like this:

y=ax+b y = ax + b

In the equation above, aa and bb are constants that determine the shape of the line. aa represents the slope of the line, which corresponds visually to how steep is the line. Similarly, bb is called the y-intercept and represents the point where the line crosses the yy axis.
